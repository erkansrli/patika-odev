Profiteröl Recipe

For Profiterole :

    For the Dough;
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 glass of water
  • 100 g butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 eggs
    • For the Cream:
  • 2.5 cups of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of starch
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • vanilla with the tip of the knife
    • For the Sauce:
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of starch
  • 200 g dark chocolate

Preparation

For the balls, put water, salt and butter in the pot and bring it to a boil, After boiling, take it from the stove and add the flour and mix quickly, When the flour and water are completely mixed, take it back to the stove and cook on low heat for 5 minutes, stirring constantly, Take it from the stove and wait for it to cool, Add one of the eggs and beat until you get a smooth dough, Do the same with the other eggs one by one, You will get a liquid dough that will not spread when you squeeze it into the tray, Put the dough in a piping bag and squeeze 3 cm balls at intervals on the tray on which you laid baking paper, Bake the balls in the oven heated to 190 degrees until they swell and turn brown, After cooking, do not take it out of the oven until it resets the heat and cools down, For the cream, put all the ingredients except butter and vanilla in the pot and mix them and cook until they get a consistency, Add the butter and vanilla and cook, stirring, until the butter melts and remove from the heat, Stir at regular intervals so that it does not form a crust on it until it cools down, For the sauce, take the milk in a saucepan, add the starch and whisk, Add the chocolate you cut into small pieces and take it to the stove, Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the chocolate melts and reaches the consistency of sauce, Take it off the fire and stir it at regular intervals until it cools down, Put the cream in a piping bag or food injector, squeeze into balls and place on serving plates, Drizzle the chocolate sauce over it.
